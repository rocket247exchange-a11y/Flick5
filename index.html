What is a FLICK® ?

FLICK® is an experimental African storytelling universe — born from a passion to merge art, culture, and artificial intelligence. Each short film (1–3 minutes) pulls you into a dramatic blend of cinematic visuals and immersive sound. Every episode tells a new story — mythical, romantic, horrifying, or beautifully strange.
